Evaluation Access

This repository provides a public, demonstration-only representation of a dual-channel fail-closed validation model.

Access to full system capabilities, including validation control layers, execution governance mechanisms, and production-grade architecture, is restricted.

---

Evaluation Pathway

Enterprise evaluation access may be granted under controlled conditions, including:

- Non-disclosure agreement (NDA)
- Defined evaluation scope
- Restricted environment access
- No replication or reverse engineering

---

Pilot Programs

Pilot deployments may be available for:

- high-assurance environments
- AI validation and governance systems
- security-critical infrastructure

All pilots are conducted under formal agreement and controlled access conditions.

---

Restrictions

The following are not permitted without explicit authorization:

- replication of system design
- reverse engineering of architecture
- simulation of validation mechanisms
- commercial use of concepts or outputs
